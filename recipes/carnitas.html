Carnitas

Description

A very easy, extremely savory family dinner that will bring the authentic taste of mexico right at home!

Ingredients

  • Coke/Dr.Pepper
  • Pork butt roast
  • Salt
  • Vegetable oil

Directions

  1. Add pork butt roast to a bowl and then add can of coke and let it soak overnight.
  2. Cut Roast into large chunks.
  3. Preheat the oil in the pan.
  4. Fry all of the side of the butt roast chunks, browning each side individually.
  5. Add the fried chunks into the slow cooker and coat each piece with salt as you individually add to slow cooker.
  6. Cook on High for 5-6 Hours checking until its tender enough to shred.
  7. Shred the meat with forks or meat claws and enjoy!
