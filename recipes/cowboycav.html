Cowboy Caviar

Description

Tasteful, elegant, and scrumptious chip dip that will compliment your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 orange bell pepper
  • 1 yellow bell pepper
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 2 jalapenos
  • 3/4 red onion
  • Can of sliced black peppers
  • 1 mango
  • 1 container of feta cheese
  • 2 avocados
  • 2 cans of rinsed black beans
  • 1 can of rinsed pinto beans
  • 2 cups of frozen sweet corn

Dressing:

  • Juice of 3 limes
  • 1/2 cup of olive oil
  • vinegar
  • 3-4 tbsp taco seasoning
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 2-4 tbsp spicy honey
  • 3tbps Sugar

Directions

  1. Dice everything and combine in a bowl
  2. Mix dressing in seperate bowl
  3. Add the dressing to diced veggies and mix together
