Crockpot Buffalo Wild Wings Garlic Parmesan Chicken

Description

Easy and tasteful family dinner with a kick!

Ingredients

  • 2-3 Chicken breast
  • Buffalo Wild Wings parmesan garlic sauce
  • Milk(Whole)
  • Cream Cheese (Cubed)
  • Cut permesan cheese
  • Pasta

Directions

  1. Spray/Line crockpot.
  2. Add chicken breast.
  3. Add whole bottle of Buffalo Wild Wings parmesan garlic sauce.
  4. Fill bottle of Buffalo Wild Wings parmesan garlic sauce with milk and shake it all up and add to crockpot.
  5. Add cubed block of cream cheese
  6. Add parmesan, salt, and pepper
  7. Cook on Low for 8 hours or on High for 4 hours.
  8. shred and serve with noodles or rice
